About Us

Located just steps from the world-famous Las Vegas Strip, The Lumina Stay is your serene escape amid the city’s vibrant energy. Our hotel offers the perfect blend of modern sophistication and heartfelt hospitality, creating a refreshing retreat for every traveler.

More than just a place to rest, The Lumina Stay is your home base for unforgettable experiences. Whether you're here to explore, entertain, or unwind, we provide the light-filled comfort and convenience you deserve.

Amenities

After a day exploring the thrills of Las Vegas, return to comfort and care at The Lumina Stay. We offer thoughtful amenities to elevate your stay, including high-speed Wi-Fi, in-room dining, laundry services, and a knowledgeable concierge team ready to assist with local recommendations.

Designed for both convenience and calm, our amenities ensure you’re well-rested and ready to experience the best of Vegas, day after day.
