Cookie Policy for The Lumina Stay

This Cookie Policy describes how The Lumina Stay ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website (the "Website"). It outlines what cookies are, how we use them, and the choices available to you regarding their use.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember user actions and preferences, enabling smoother and more personalized interactions.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure proper functioning of the Website: Essential cookies support features like secure login and navigation.

  • Understand user behavior: Analytics cookies help us assess site performance and user engagement.

  • Customize user experience: Preference cookies store your selected settings, such as language or region.

  • Serve targeted advertising: Marketing cookies track your activity to provide relevant promotional content.

Categories of Cookies We Use

We categorize the cookies we use as follows:

  • Essential Cookies: These are crucial for basic site operations and security. Disabling them may affect functionality.

  • Analytics Cookies: These track site traffic and usage patterns. We use tools like Google Analytics to gather this data.

  • Marketing Cookies: These support personalized ads and campaigns. Tools such as Facebook Pixel and Google Ads may be used for this purpose.

Your Cookie Choices

You control how cookies are used:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies. Refer to your browser’s help section for guidance.

  • Opt-Out Options: We may offer tools on our Website or link to third-party opt-outs for marketing and analytics cookies.

Compliance with EU Cookie Regulations

We comply with GDPR and ePrivacy Directive by:

  • Providing transparent cookie usage information.

  • Requesting consent before using non-essential cookies.

  • Allowing users to withdraw consent at any time.

Updates to this Cookie Policy

We may revise this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. Please review this page regularly to stay informed about our cookie usage.
